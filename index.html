Got questions?

  • (Q1)

    Who is this for?

    I work with companies built on expertise — consultants, studios, firms, and specialists. The kind of businesses where trust and reputation really drive results. If your work is strong but your website doesn’t show it, I help close that gap so your online presence actually matches your level.

  • (Q2)

    Who is this not for?

    If you’re after “Apple vibes” on a ramen budget, or hoping a redesign will revive a business that hasn’t had clients in years, I’ll have to pass. Also, If you’re after quick fixes or cheap tricks that promise to quadruple conversions overnight, I’m not your guy. But if you want something thoughtful, real, and built to last — that’s where I come in.

  • (Q3)

    Do you only do design, or development as well?

    Both, so you get a complete, ready-to-use website. I handle everything from start to finish and deliver a finished product that’s ready to go live.

  • (Q4)

    How much does a website cost?

    It really depends on the size and complexity of your project, but most websites I design and build land somewhere between $2.5K and $8K USD.

  • (Q5)

    How long does it take to create a website?

    Most projects take around 4–8 weeks, depending on size and feedback speed. Smaller sites are usually done in about a month, while bigger builds can take closer to two. You’ll always know the timeline upfront, and I’ll keep the process clear and simple along the way.

  • (Q6)

    Do you guarantee results?

    Absolutely! I’ll create a website that attracts the right clients, highlights your expertise, and earns genuine trust — all before a single conversation happens. You’ll have a strong online presence that feels effortless, brings better opportunities, and truly represents the quality of your work. Everything under my control is guaranteed.

    If you’re asking about sales... well, I’m not a Hogwarts graduate. I still haven’t learned a magic spell that summons clients out of thin air. But it takes less than ten minutes to find a designer who’ll promise you that.

  • (Q7)

    How do we start?

    If this sounds like something that fits, just fill up the form here or email me at hello@bogdankolomiyets.com. We’ll have a quick chat to see if we’re a good fit.

    If there is — great, we move forward. If not, no hard feelings.
